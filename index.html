Snake Game

Instructions

Use the arrow keys (↑, →, ↓, ←) to control the direction of the snake

Your goal is to eat the fruits that appear on the board

Each 🥝 you eat will increase your score by 10 points and 🍊 by 20 points

If your timer reaches 0 or the snake collides with a wall or itself, the game is over

Try to achieve the highest score and beat your best score!
